Kanban: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
☻ Team picks up work as capacity allows. ☹ Harder to plan out and project large release initiatives.
☻ Less process heavy, more flexible process. ☹ May cause poor productivity.
☻ Focused on continuous delivery. ☹ Easier to misuse process.
☻ Great for teams with many requests. ☹ Can be difficult to manage different priorities/ influx of work.
