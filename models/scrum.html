Scrum: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
☻ Creates accountability. ☹ Can lead to scope creep if no end-date is defined.
☻ Clear definition of 'results'. ☹ Harder to manage in large teams.
☻ Accomodates change to plans. ☹ Changes to team structure creates large impacts.
☻ Allows for planning of large initiatives/ better metrics. ☹ Requires team buy-in, which in turn can cause frustrations if the full team is not alighned with approach.
☻ Use time and money most effectively. ☹ Velocity can be easily skewed depending on team and product variables.
